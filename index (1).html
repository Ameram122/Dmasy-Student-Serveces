About Us

Our mission is to make international education accessible to students from all backgrounds.

Our Story

Founded in 2013, DMASY Student Services began with a simple goal: to help students navigate the complex process of studying abroad. What started as a small consultancy in Tbilisi has grown into a trusted name in international education, assisting thousands of students from across the globe.

For English-medium programs, most universities require proof of English proficiency (like IELTS 5.5-6.0 or equivalent). Some universities offer preparatory English courses if you don't meet the requirements. For programs taught in Georgian or Russian, language courses are typically available before starting your degree.

The cost of living is relatively affordable. In Tbilisi, students typically spend $300-$500 per month including accommodation, food, and transportation. In Tashkent, costs are slightly lower at $250-$400 per month. Shared apartments are the most economical housing option, while university dormitories are even more budget-friendly.
